About Us

At StudyTrack, we believe that managing your studies and finances shouldn’t be stressful. Our mission is to empower students to stay organized, achieve their goals, and make smarter financial decisions—all in one intuitive app.
With features like study planners, exam reminders, expense tracking, and personalized insights, we help students take control of their time and money. We’re dedicated to creating a simple, powerful, and beautiful tool that makes student life easier and more productive.
Our Vision: To be the go-to platform for students who want to study smarter, spend wiser, and live stress-free.
